Call For Papers

BSidesHBG is inviting cybersecurity researchers, professionals, and everyone with special knowledge to present their skills, tools, and experience.

  • Code of Conduct

We are looking for submissions related to Information Security. Present your research, tools, techniques or other ideas you would like to share with the local InfoSec community.

InfoSec Topics (not limited to):

  • Information Security & Privacy
  • Network Security
  • Cryptography
  • Application Security
  • DevSecOps
  • Database Security
  • Penetration Testing and Vulnerability Analysis
  • Security Engineering and Management
  • Digital Forensics & Incident Response (DFIR)
  • Mobile Security
  • Virtualization and Cloud Security
  • Offensive/Defensive Security
  • Cyber Operations
  • ICS/SCADA Systems
  • IOT Devices
  • Governance, Risk, & Compliance (GRC)
  • No advertising or sales pitches.
