Code of Conduct

BSidesHarrisburg is an inclusive and professional community.

BSidesHBG is composed of a community of professionals and it is expected that everyone who attends the event will conduct themselves in a professional manner. We aspire to be a welcoming place for everyone. Any amount of insult, harassment, inappropriate or unlawful behavior will most likely result in being asked to immediately leave the conference by the staff of the venue or organizers of the conference. Some examples of things that are unacceptable include:

  • Sexist, racist, homophobic, transphobic, or otherwise discriminatory behavior, uncouth language or jokes
  • Displaying violent or sexually explicit material
  • Displaying or posting other people's personally identifying information

If you experience or witness any behavior that conflicts with our Code of Conduct, please contact a volunteer, venue staff, and/or email organizers(at)bsideshbg.com.
